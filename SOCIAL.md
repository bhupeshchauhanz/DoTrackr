🚀 **Shipped DoTrackr — A habit & todo tracker I built from scratch in Flutter!**

Not a tutorial project. Not a clone. Just a problem I wanted to solve.

I wanted a habit tracker that actually *feels* premium and works exactly how my brain works:

✅ **Smart Push Notifications** — battery-friendly reminders using standard push notifications. Missed a habit? A daily 11 PM summary notification ensures you never forget to complete your tasks.

📊 **Statistics Dashboard** — weekly, monthly, and yearly consistency heatmaps (month-by-month like GitHub), completion timeline, habit breakdown with progress bars, and a trophy room for your best streak.

💾 **Local Backup & Restore** — export your entire data as a JSON file to your Downloads folder. Reinstall? Just import and everything comes back instantly.

🎨 **Premium Dark UI** — glassmorphism, micro-animations, Inter typeface, everything snappy.

Tech stack: Flutter 3 · Riverpod · Hive · flutter_local_notifications · fl_chart
